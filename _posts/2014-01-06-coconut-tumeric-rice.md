title: Coconut Tumeric Rice
---


* 3/4 c white rice
* 1/2 t turmeric
* 1 t vegetable oil
* 1/4 t salt
* 3/4 c water
* 1/3 c coconut milk

Rinse and drain the rice

In a saucepan on medium heat, saut√© the rice, turmeric, and salt in the vegetable oil for a minute or two, stirring constantly

Add the water and coconut milk, bring to a boil, cover and reduce the heat to low

Simmer until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed, about 15 to 20 minutes
