title: Asian Slaw Dressing
subtitle: a la Noodle Express
---

> We usually have this over shredded cabbage, grated carrots, and half-rounds of cucumber. 


* 1/2 c canola oil
* 1/2 c white vinegar
* 1/4 c white sugar
* 1 T sesame seeds
* 1 T dijon mustard
* 1 t sesame oil 
* 1/4 t ground pepper
* 1/4 t salt

Blend ingredients for 30 seconds.
